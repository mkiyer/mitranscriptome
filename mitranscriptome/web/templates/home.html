About

The MiTranscriptome was assembled from an ab initio transcriptome construction based on 6,503 RNA-seq libraries from multiple cancer and tissue types. The lncRNAs most differentially expressed in cancers and tissues are reported in this database. A novel non-parametric tool, called Sample Set Enrichment Analysis (SSEA) was used to perform differential expression analysis. MiTranscriptome was developed at the Michigan Center for Translational Pathology at the University of Michigan.
