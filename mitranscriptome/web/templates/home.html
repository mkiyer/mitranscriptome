Welcome to MiTranscriptome!

MiTranscriptome is a comprehensive tool that allows researchers to study gene expression across multiple cancer-related sample sets, providing powerful insight into the cancer transcriptome.

Our analysis has identified Lineage-Associated Transcripts (LATs), Cancer-Associated Transcripts (CATs), and a combination of Cancer- and Lineage-Associated Transcripts (CLATs). We have also identified a subset of Highly Conserved lncRNAs (HiCLNCs). Finally, we have included a list of previously annotated lncRNAs for your browsing pleasure.
